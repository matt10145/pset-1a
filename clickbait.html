David's Clickbait Brand

Clickbait, Dobrik's main tagline, now represents his series of merchandise. On the website fanjoy.co, Dobrik sells a variety of Clickbait-themed clothing, including undergarments, t-shirts, hoodies, and sweatpants. In September 2019, Dobrik revealed "The Beverly Collection," a line of colorful hoodies with "David Dobrik" printed on each one. Some may say that this was a step away from the usual "Clickbait" clothing.
