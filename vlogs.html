The foundation of Dobrik's fame: short, fast-paced, and comedic vlogs. Dobrik has posted for about four years now, with the videos becoming bigger and more exciting each time. Each vlog normally consists of short 10-30 second comedy bits, with most of the Vlog Squad members playing specific characters regularly. For instance, Dom Zeglaitis, one of David's older friends, plays the role of a very erratic, drug-addicted man. Jason Nash naturally fits in as the older, wiser friend who tags along in the fun and occasionally watches for everyone's safety. Corinna Kopf plays the high-energy, promiscuous girlfriend in the group who is in a relationship with the manly and attractive Toddy Smith.

Some of my favorite vlogs include:

  1. "I MARRIED MY BEST FRIENDS MOM!!! (PROPOSAL)"
  2. "SURPRISING BEST FRIEND WITH DREAM BIRTHDAY!!"
  3. "FRIENDS SURPRISE ME FOR 10 MILLION SUBSCRIBERS!!"
  4. "HE SHOULD NOT HAVE DONE THIS!! (BAD IDEA)"
  5. "THIS SHOULD NOT HAVE HAPPENED!!"
  6. ...and many more!

A theme Dobrik tends to implement in the vlogs is giving back. He emphasizes this in his numerous car giveaways from over the last year and a half. Dobrik partners with the ticketing company SeatGeek on nearly every one of them -- asking SeatGeek for money has now become a running joke in the vlogs.

All of the car giveaways as of September 2019 have been directed towards the Vlog Squad friend group. However, one sweet, poignant giveaway featured high school senior Dylan Corn, whom David had met randomly at a Target.
